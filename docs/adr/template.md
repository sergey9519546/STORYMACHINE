## Context

What situation forced this decision? State the problem, the constraints
that bound it, and any measurements or evidence that frame it. Write it so
a reader with no session context understands why doing nothing was not an
option.

## Decision

The choice made, stated plainly in one or two sentences, followed by the
essential mechanics of how it works.

## Alternatives considered

For each rejected option: what it was, and the concrete reason it lost.
"We didn't like it" is not a reason; a measured or structural tradeoff is.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, what becomes harder, and what future work this
commits us to or forecloses. Include the honest costs — an ADR that lists
only upsides is marketing, not a record.
