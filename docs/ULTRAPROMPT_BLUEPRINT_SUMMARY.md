### STAGE 6: THE THREE QUESTIONS
Existential choices that determine which blueprint paths to pursue:

**1. Mirror or Teacher?**
- Mirror: shows structure, zero judgment, creates self-aware writers
- Teacher: prescribes fixes, creates dependent writers, Goodhart's Law risk

**2. Judge or Simulator?**
- Judge: analyzes screenplay text as pure narrative (serves writers/dev execs)
- Simulator: models entire production pipeline including costs (serves producers/studios)

**3. Universal or Pluralistic?**
- Universal: one correct analysis exists, reader disagreement is error
- Pluralistic: multiple valid readings coexist, disagreement is data

Each answer determines which of 14 vectors to build first and which to abandon entirely.
