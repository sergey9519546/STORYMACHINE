## The Three Existential Questions (Must Answer Before Building)

### Question 1: Mirror or Teacher?
- **Mirror:** Shows structural facts, zero prescription, creates self-aware writers
- **Teacher:** Prescribes fixes, creates tool-dependent writers, risks Goodhart's Law

**Implication:** Determines UI language (observational vs. prescriptive), feature priorities, and long-term writer relationship.

### Question 2: Judge or Simulator?
- **Judge:** Analyzes screenplay as pure narrative (serves writers/dev execs, subscription model)
- **Simulator:** Models production pipeline with costs/risks (serves producers/studios, underwriting model)

**Implication:** Determines customer, business model, and which capabilities get prioritized.

### Question 3: Universal or Pluralistic?
- **Universal:** One correct reading exists, benchmark seeks ground truth, appeals to rationalists
- **Pluralistic:** Multiple valid readings coexist, benchmark preserves disagreement, appeals to humanists

**Implication:** Determines validation methodology, how reader disagreement is treated, and brand positioning (scientific instrument vs. cultural mirror).
