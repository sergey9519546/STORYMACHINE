## Red Flags (Who NOT to Schedule)

❌ **People who want to pitch their script**: "Can you also give me notes on my concept?"  
❌ **People who want free coverage**: "Will you read the whole thing and give detailed notes?"  
❌ **People who are defensive**: "My structure is perfect, I just want validation"  
❌ **People who haven't written yet**: "I'm planning to write a screenplay soon"  
❌ **People trying to sell you something**: "I also run a coverage service, let's partner"  

✅ **Good candidates**: "I'm revising a draft and would love honest feedback on a structural tool"

## Target: 8-10 Writers

**Diversity goals**:
- 3-4 emerging writers (first screenplay, early in career)
- 3-4 working writers (sold scripts, produced work)
- 1-2 established writers (multiple credits)
- Mix of genres (drama, thriller, comedy, sci-fi, etc.)
- Mix of draft stages (rough draft, polished draft, rewrite)

**Why diversity matters**: Different writers value different things. Emerging writers may love any feedback. Working writers may be more skeptical. Need both perspectives.

---

## Key Success Metrics

From each session, track:
- ✅ Strong pull (asked how to access, took notes, enthusiastic yes)
- 🟡 Weak pull (said "interesting," would "maybe try")
- ❌ No pull (didn't engage, skeptical, said no)

**Target**: 4+ strong pull out of 8-10 = GREEN LIGHT (proceed to P1)
