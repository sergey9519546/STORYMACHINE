# P0 User Validation - Recruitment Outreach Templates

## Overview

These templates support recruiting 5+ real screenwriters for P0 validation sessions. The goal is observing whether the existing sample coverage report creates genuine pull toward running their own draft.

---

## Template 1: Cold Outreach (Writing Communities / Forums)

**Subject:** Quick feedback on a screenplay analysis tool?

**Body:**

Hi [Name / Community],

I'm working on a tool that gives screenwriters instant, private feedback on their drafts—similar to what a professional reader would provide, but automated.

I'm looking for 5-10 screenwriters who'd be willing to spend 20-30 minutes looking at a sample report and sharing honest feedback. No sales pitch, no account required—just showing you what it does and asking if it's useful.

**What I'm looking for:**
- You have at least one screenplay draft (any stage, any genre)
- You're willing to share honest reactions (good or bad)
- 20-30 minute video call at your convenience

**What you get:**
- Free analysis of your own script (if you want it)
- Early access to the tool when it launches
- Direct input into what gets built

Interested? Reply or DM me and I'll send a calendar link.

Thanks,
[Your Name]

[Your Name]

## Template 5: Follow-Up After Initial Interest

**Subject:** Re: Screenplay analysis tool feedback

**Body:**

Hey [Name],

Thanks for your interest in providing feedback on the screenplay tool!

Here's what the session looks like:

**Format:** 20-30 minute video call (Zoom, Google Meet, or your preference)

**What happens:**
1. I show you a sample coverage report for a screenplay (not yours)
2. You browse it naturally—no script, no guidance
3. I ask questions about what you found useful, what you trusted, what you'd ignore
4. We discuss whether you'd want to run your own draft through it

**What I'm NOT doing:**
- Pitching you on the tool
- Asking you to buy anything
- Requiring a testimonial

**Scheduling:** [Insert calendar link or propose 2-3 times]

**Optional:** If you'd like me to analyze one of your scripts afterward (free), just send it over as a PDF after the session.

Let me know if you have any questions!

Best,
[Your Name]

## Target Channels for Recruitment

### High-Potential Channels:
1. **Reddit:** r/Screenwriting, r/Screenwriters
2. **Facebook Groups:** Screenwriting groups, Film Production groups
3. **Stage 32:** Screenwriting forums
4. **LinkedIn:** Screenwriting professionals, Film industry groups
5. **Twitter/X:** #Screenwriting, #Screenwriters hashtags
6. **Discord:** Screenwriting community servers
7. **Film School Alumni Groups** (if applicable)
8. **Local Filmmaker Meetups** (Meetup.com, Eventbrite)

### Warm Outreach:
- Personal network (anyone who writes)
- Friends of friends referrals
- Film industry contacts
- Writing workshop participants
- Contest entrants (if you have access)

---

## Recruitment Timeline

**Days 1-2:** Send outreach messages to all channels  
**Days 3-5:** Follow up with interested respondents  
**Days 4-7:** Schedule 5+ sessions  
**Week 2:** Conduct sessions  
**Week 2-3:** Document and synthesize findings

---

## Success Metrics

- **Target:** 5+ completed sessions (ROADMAP requirement)
- **Stretch:** 8-10 sessions for stronger signal
- **Diversity:** Mix of career stages (emerging, established, professional)
- **Genres:** Variety of genres (drama, comedy, thriller, etc.)

---

## Notes

- Be upfront about time commitment (20-30 min)
- Don't oversell the tool—honesty matters more
- Make it easy to say no
- Offer tangible value (free analysis, early access)
- Follow up promptly with interested respondents
