## Incorporation queue (deduplicated across all six audits, ranked by
## fit with the live engine's wave program)

### Tier 1 — deterministic rules ready to spec (wave-sized, corpus-measurable)
- **Coincidence-direction rule** (Pixar): coincidence may create problems,
  never resolve them; + 5% typed orphan/coincidence budget with climax
  exemption (Ernie V5.55) as guards on causality.ts.
- **RelationshipRepairProof**: repair requires acknowledgement AND cost AND
  changed behavior AND victim-visible evidence ("bad apology" detector).
- **Reincorporation density**: % of Act-3 elements planted earlier AND
  state-changed; >=70% excellence twin / orphaned-setup defect twin.
- **Dialogue-information ratio** (<=40% of dramatic info via dialogue) +
  anti-sentimentality (stated feeling without mechanism).
- **Burrows's Delta voice differentiation** (function-word z-scores,
  alert <0.7 for 3 scenes) — deterministic, no embeddings.
- **Effort-supremacy / first-try-success flag**: min 3 failure cycles
  before transformation; want-need opposition validator; antagonist
  defensibility (No Pure Evil) check.
- **Silence/Letting-Go excellence detectors**: silent-or-minimal climax;
  release-vs-victory resolution classifier; object-arc-carries-theme.
- **Oxytocin window**: no post-climax settling beat >= ~1 page -> flag.
- **Dialogue attribution at scale**: tag-overload/lost-speaker detectors.
- **Mirror-scene detection** + Scene Transition taxonomy (thematic rhyme /
  ironic juxtaposition / emotional bridge) — UltraAnalysis's never-built #3.

### Tier 2 — signal channels (Type 1 waves)
