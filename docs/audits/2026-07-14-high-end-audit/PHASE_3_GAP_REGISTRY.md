# Phase 3 — Implementation-Ready Gap Registry

**Audit snapshot:** 2026-07-14/15. **Decision rule:** executable behavior and
direct inspection outrank prose; a green test suite does not compensate for a
failed content-integrity, confidentiality, claim-validity, legal-permission, or
demand gate. This registry consolidates every verified Critical/Major finding
and groups lower-severity operational debt where one control and exit test can
close it.

## Evidence and disposition legend

- **EX** — executed witness; **DC** — direct code/config/DOM trace; **XR** —
  cross-artifact/source convergence; **LR** — bounded external literature or
  official guidance. `Certain` means the behavior was executed or follows
  directly from the inspected implementation; it does not mean downstream
  user or legal harm was observed.
- **FIX NOW** — scoped safety, integrity, validation-readiness, or truth repair
  that does not require evidence of writer demand. **P0/P1/P2** — the durable
  change belongs behind that roadmap gate. **HOLD** — real but non-urgent work
  that must not displace gate clearing.
- Futures: **A** = Deterministic Coverage Authority; **B** = Reader Studio;
  **C** = Reflective Draft Clinic. A future reference is impact scope, not an
  endorsement or authorization.

## Priority rollup

| Disposition | Count | Execution meaning |
|---|---:|---|
| FIX NOW | 15 | Stop destructive restore, confidential build-context transfer, unbounded AI paths, false save/state outcomes, and validation-blind UI/test behavior; correct canonical truth. |
| P0 | 3 | Establish an honest private-trial posture and collect committed real-writer behavior; no product expansion. |
| P1 | 6 | Define and validate score/interpretation use on rights-cleared real writing; no score retuning before these gates. |
| P2 | 2 | Collapse and clarify the product surface only after a separately gated post-P0 experiment records a branch decision; Future A additionally requires exact P1 validity. |
| HOLD | 5 | Preserve as release/scale/service debt; schedule only when it no longer competes with P0/P1 evidence. |

**Dependency order:** exact-commit, truthfully framed, sample-only P0 can proceed
without collecting a participant draft while `G-001–G-015` are repaired in
parallel. Any own-draft/private-beta lane remains blocked by the applicable
integrity, privacy, identity, manifest, provider-consent, and accessibility
controls. P1 design may follow P0, but execution requires a later valid gate
showing need for consequential automated judgment; P2 remains behind a
separately gated post-P0 experiment decision and its roadmap gate;
release/scale investment follows the evidence and selected exposure.
