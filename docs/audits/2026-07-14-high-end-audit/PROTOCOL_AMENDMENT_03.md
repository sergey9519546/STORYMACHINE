# Protocol Amendment 03 — Exact 2026 WGA MBA Source

**Date:** 2026-07-15

**Trigger:** Phase 5 editorial, ethics, and source-faithfulness review

**Changes the research question, frozen 48-source count, or decision gates:** No
**Corrects a citation-to-claim mismatch:** Yes

## Prior citation problem

The final report's 2026 MBA notification statement cited source G12, the WGA's
separate “Artificial intelligence” page summarizing 2023 MBA protections. G12
supports the bounded propositions that AI is not a writer under the MBA, AI
output is not literary/source material, and companies must disclose
AI-generated material supplied to writers. It does not directly support the
distinct 2026 MBA contract-change notification proposition.

The claim was authentic, but the adjacent source was not the exact source for
that claim. Blending the two pages under G12 would obscure their different
dates and effects.

## Exact supplemental source

**S02 — Writers Guild of America. (2026). *2026 MBA Contract Changes FAQ*.**

Official URL:
https://www.wga.org/contracts/contracts/mba/2026-mba-contract-changes-faq

The official FAQ states, in its artificial-intelligence section, that a company
must notify the Guild when it licenses a writer's material to train a commercial
generative-AI system and that the Guild may request a meeting to discuss
appropriate remuneration. This is a bounded MBA professional-contract context,
not universal product law, copyright law, or a determination that the MBA
applies to STORYMACHINE.

## Evidentiary treatment

- **Classification:** official/professional primary source; Level VII; Grade A
  for the exact MBA contract-change proposition.
- **Incentive/context:** WGA advocates for its writer membership and explains a
  negotiated agreement. That is authoritative for the bounded agreement claim,
  not independent product-efficacy or general legal evidence.
- **Temporal scope:** current official page verified during the 2026-07-15
  Phase 5 review; refresh when the agreement, page, or proposed product use
  changes.
- **Counting rule:** S02 is a post-freeze supplemental source. It is not added
  to, and does not change, the registered 48-source Phase 2 corpus.

## Downstream correction

G12 remains the source for the 2023 MBA AI-protections summary. S02 exclusively
supports the 2026 notification/remuneration-discussion statement. The final
report, bibliography, source register, and verification record must use the S02
identifier and exact FAQ URL for that sentence, with the MBA-only and
fact-specific legal-scope qualifiers preserved.
